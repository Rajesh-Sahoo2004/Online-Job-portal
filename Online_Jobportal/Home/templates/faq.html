What is this job portal?
Our job portal is an online platform that connects job seekers with employers. It helps users find suitable job opportunities easily through advanced search filters and personalized recommendations.
Is registration free?
Yes, registration is completely free for job seekers. Employers can post up to 3 jobs for free, with premium plans available for unlimited postings.
How can I apply for a job?
Create an account, complete your profile with resume, search for jobs using filters, and click the "Apply Now" button. You can apply to multiple jobs with one click.
Can employers post jobs?
Yes, employers can register and post job openings. Free accounts get 3 job postings per month, while premium accounts offer unlimited postings and priority visibility.
How can I contact support?
Use our Contact page, email support@jobportal.com, or call +1 (555) 123-4567. Response time is typically within 24 hours.
How do I update my profile?
Go to "My Profile" in your dashboard, click "Edit Profile," upload your updated resume, and save changes. Profile updates are live immediately.
Can I apply without a resume?
Most jobs require a resume for better matching, but some entry-level positions allow applications with just profile information.
How do I track my applications?
Visit "My Applications" in your dashboard to see application status (Applied, Shortlisted, Interview, Rejected) with real-time updates from employers.
How long do job postings stay active?
Free postings stay active for 30 days. Premium postings remain active until you close them or hire a candidate.
